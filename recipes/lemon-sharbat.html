Lemon Sharbat Recipe

Description

This is a basic lemon sharbat recipe for this project.

Ingredients

  • One whole Lemon juice
  • Honey
  • Water
  • Sabja(Basil seeds)
  • Mint leaves(optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the lemon juice with some honey and stir well.
  2. Add it to a glass with some cold water and ice if needed.
  3. Put in some sabja and let it soak for a minute.
  4. Top your drink with mint leaves and Rejoice!
