Coffee Recipe

Description

This is my very personal coffee recipe that I enjoy even if I have it everyday! Happy to share!

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee Powder
  • Honey
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Keep milk to heat up.
  2. While that is happening, Add coffee and honey to your cup as needed.
  3. Add some water and stir well.
  4. Finally add milk and enjoy. If needed add more milk or honey.
