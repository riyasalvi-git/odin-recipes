Tea Recipe

Description

This is my very personal Tea recipe that I enjoy even if I have it everyday! Happy to share!

Ingredients

  • Black Tea Powder
  • Honey
  • Milk
  • Water

Steps

  1. Keep milk to heat up.
  2. On other side boil water and add tea powder to it.
  3. Let it boil for a minute and the turn of the heat and cover the water for about 10 mins. Longer if you like your tea strong.
  4. Strain the tea in your favourite mug, add honey and stir well.
  5. Finally add milk and enjoy. If needed add more milk or honey.
